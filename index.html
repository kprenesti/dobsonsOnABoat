About Magen's Bay Beach

Marian and Steven will be wed on the shore of Magen's Bay Beach, St. Thomas. Magen's Bay is considered one of the Caribbean's top ten beaches. It is known for its crystal clear, calm waters and white sandy beaches. Magen's Bay is the perfect location for a morning wedding, an afternoon swim, sunbathing and/or people watching with a tropical drink. (Yes, there is a bar and a small restaurant available on the beach.) As a wedding guest, your admission to the beach is paid, so if you would like to remain on the beach until the end of the day, please feel free. Otherwise, feel free to book an excursion that leaves later in the day (probably no earlier than twelve or twelve-thirty to be safe.)

Wedding FAQs

Below you will find some of the most frequently asked questions. Click on the question to reveal the answer. If you have further questions, please don't hesitate to ask!

Where and When?

The wedding will be held on Magens Bay Beach at approximately 10AM on May 10th, 2016. It will be directly on the beach.

What should I wear?

Since the wedding will be directly on the beach, and since guests may want to enjoy the beach and/or another excursion of their choice, guests should wear dressy but comfortable beach clothes. There will be places to change on the beach, so whether or not you wear your bathing suit under your clothes is up to you.

How long will the wedding last?

It should last less than an hour and will start around 10AM.

What should I bring?

This question depends on whether or not you intend to return to the ship, go directly to an alternate excursion, or stay on Magens Bay. Regardless of your choice, you need to bring cash for transportation. In order to accommodate individual plans for the day, transportation back to the ship will not be provided. If you intend to stay on the beach, bring a towel from the ship, sunscreen, and anything else you think you'd like to have on the beach.

Will transportation be provided?

One-way transportation to the wedding site will be provided but transportation back to the ship will not be provided since guests can do whatever they want after the wedding and before the reception back on the ship.

Who can come to the ceremony and the reception?

If you would like to witness the wedding, your presence will be most welcome.
